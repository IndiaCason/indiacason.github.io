Design Philosophy

How I design learning

Grounded in ADDIE and adult learning principles, every project starts with the learner's real workflow and ends with measurable performance change.

01
Analyze

Training needs analysis, stakeholder interviews, workflow assessments, and GAP analysis to define the performance gap — before touching any authoring tool.

02
Design

Learning objectives mapped to competencies. Storyboards, interaction design, and course architecture planned before a single slide is built.

03
Develop

eLearning built in Articulate 360 or Adobe Captivate; ILT/VILT facilitation guides; job aids and SOPs; video walkthroughs in Camtasia.

04
Implement

LMS deployment, learner enrollment, communication planning, and live facilitation — including train-the-trainer programs for large rollouts.

05
Evaluate

Learning analytics, completion dashboards, post-training knowledge assessments, and feedback loops that inform continuous improvement.
